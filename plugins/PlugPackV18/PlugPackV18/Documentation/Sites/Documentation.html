Documentation
 
This plug-in package contains 10 different plug-ins.
Non of these plugs depends on another so you can freely choose the ones you want to play with.
 
Plug-in Description
   
ExtraOutfits v18 Part 1 Choose from 150 new balanced outfits (i. e. Bank Accounts, Starbridge D/E upgrades, Fake ID's, Inertial Damper, Crew Quarters, Officers, Weapon Construction Bay, Vell-os abilities, new weapon types, and a bunch of other exotic new technologies) to equip your ship and get three new types of ships (new graphics) to escort you as well as one new ship (new graphics) for own use.
   
ExtraOutfits v18 Part 2 Buy your own shipyard in the Spica system and produce ships which you are able to sell or use for your own desires. This plug also includes a new ship for own use (new graphics) which can be found on the Spica Shipyard as well.
   
NewCharacters v8 Tired to always begin with this tiny Shuttle? Choose from 23 different starting characters and start playing the game your way.
   
NewTargets v3 This plug replaces the red target pictures in EVN with colored ones.
   
NewMissions v4 Get to Las Vegas and become a member of the GRN to fly three different types of racing missions and push your income.
   
ReturnToS7evyn v2 Finished a storyline and missed to get a Kestrel? This will no longer happen. Put in this plug-in and you will instantly get access to a Stargate System which links S7evyn and the Obatta System.
   
TechnicalData v1 Ever wondered how much armor capacity Carbon Fibre adds or how much damage the IR Missile does? By using this plug-in you will get detailed ingame description information about any outfit available.
   
PaintStation v1 Tired of the old look of your ship? Get to the Paint Station in the Zel system and give it a paint job with one of 17 different colors.
   
ShieldBubbles v1 This plug adds shield bubbles to every non-alien ship in the game. There are 7 different shield types (one for each faction), all of which are unique from each other.
   
ShipVariants v2 This plug-in allows you to buy every variant of every ship in Nova, instead of having to capture them. All the variants have descriptions which explain their modifications. Of course, you have to have access to the regular ship class in order to be able to buy its variants. All the variant fighter bays are now available, too, with the same restrictions as above.
   
Compatible Plugs: There are no compatible plugs available!
Please do not use any other plugs with the PlugPack to prevent bugs and broken pilot files!
